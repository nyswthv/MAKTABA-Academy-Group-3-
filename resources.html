External Resources & Useful Links

Here you will find a curated list of external websites and tools that can further assist you with academic writing, research, citation, and general library knowledge. These resources are highly recommended by Maktaba Academy.

Citation & Writing Guides:

Purdue OWL (Online Writing Lab) - APA Style

The definitive online guide for APA (American Psychological Association) style, covering formatting, in-text citations, and reference lists.

Purdue OWL - MLA Style

Comprehensive guide for MLA (Modern Language Association) style, commonly used in humanities.

Chicago Manual of Style Online - Citation Guide

Official quick guide for Chicago style citations, popular in history and and arts.

Scribbr APA Citation Generator

An easy-to-use tool to automatically generate APA style citations for various source types.

Library & Research Tools:

WorldCat

A global catalog of library collections, helping you find books and other materials in libraries worldwide.

Google Scholar

A freely accessible web search engine that indexes the full text or metadata of scholarly literature across an array of publishing formats and disciplines.

Library of Congress

The largest library in the world, offering vast collections and resources for research.

Always double-check information from external sources against your specific assignment guidelines or institutional requirements.
