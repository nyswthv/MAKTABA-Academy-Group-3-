MAKTABA Academy

Where it all begins, Maktaba Academy was built in July 2014. It established digital academic library that has vision for the young generation to develop. Maktaba starting with its literacy skills such as references & citations skills. These services actually help students in enhancing their report writing. Copyright and IP are crucial in giving credits to its owner of works. Guidelines such as on how to do citations for various materials & resources are crucial in research paper and that are very helpful for students and even lecturers or researchers in doing their works. We provided beyond academic performance which are on copyright and intellectual property education that will protect people from plagiarism a deeper appreciation for the ownership of creative works within academic community. Maktaba Academy has proven the writing report of users when they started using our website back on 2015. There was the year that our website began to shine around academic community. They become more confident with their works since they are not using someone's else works without permission. Their contents are free from plagiarism that might affected their performance, but ever since we have provided these services to them, it becomes easier, honest and original work form its owner. Furthermore, many successful programs were being held online by Maktaba Academy such as literacy skills activity which can conduct through an online session. Maktaba Academy thrive to collab with more partnerships with school, community centers and online platforms to reach to a wider audience that we are able to help for their bright future. All in all, Maktaba Academy goals are to ensure future youth development and engagement in a lifelong learning which will bring inspirations to the next generation ahead. July 2, 2025
